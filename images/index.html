SALTA Infrastructure and Construction Company Limited, is a company incorporated to offer high value construction work, investment managment and concession; and serve as a special purpose vehicle for projects of varying magnitudes to range of clients in various sector of the economy.

In order to provide maximum support to our client in a schedule driven and effective environment, we have a well-established office in Lagos, Dubai, Hamburg and Houston, Texas.

Our Nigeria Offices handles business operations within Africa, Europe and the Middle East, while our U.S. Office serves as a hub for major business operations in the Americas and Far East.
